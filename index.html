FizzBuzz

How To Play:

 First, you will be prompted to enter a number that you will play to. Starting from 1 through the number you choose. For each number, you must determine if that number is a multiple of 3, a multiple of 5, or a multiple of 15.

 If it is a multiple of 3, you will enter 'fizz'. For multiples of 5, enter 'buzz'. For multiples of 3 & 5 enter 'fizzbuzz'. And if the number is not a multiple of any of those, enter the number. The goal is to correctly guess your way through each and every number and make it to the end.
